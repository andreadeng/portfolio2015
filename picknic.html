YUMI Mobile App
Helps you find someone to eat with near you, instantaneously.
Eat together, eat better, be happier
Features
Streamlined Process
Quickly find someone to eat with
Customizable
Customize the max distance you can travel for a meal, add languages you speak, and add preferred friends or groups that rank higher when YUMI searches a platemate for you. Edit your profile to reflect the foods you love and your interests!
Future Plans
We pitched YUMI to a panel of VC's at the end of the Fall 2015 semester and we're well received. We are currently in the process of developing this app. We recently decided to gamify YUMI and are working on enriching that experience as we test it out with uses. We are hoping to launch a downloadable MVP by March 2016.
Lessons Learned
I learned about the business side of product development and how the design process fits into the overall product development process. I had to adapt the design of YUMI to our business model. We planned to monetize with native ads within the app and a subscription model free of ads and unlocked features for subscribed users. Unlocked features included more searches for a platemate, preferred friends list, and groups list. The challenge was add these benefits without compensating the core product of YUMI which was finding someone to eat with instantaneously.
