VIA Marketing Material
Some flier and infographic designs I did for my Summer 2014 internship at Stanford University's VIA XSEL. VIA stands for Volunteers in Asia and XSEL stands for Exchange for Social Entrepreneurs and Leaders. I was a design coordinator for the 3 week program where mostly college students and some young professionals from all over Asia stayed in the dorms and learned about design thinking and applied that to creating a social enterprise final team project. They identified a social problem and worked in groups to solve it and present it.
